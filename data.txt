What should be the main entities in your database (which will translate into tables)?
Aim for at least three, but not more than five, to keep things from getting overly
complicated. 
What attributes (column names) belong to each of those entities? It may be helpful
to consider an example of a single record belonging to that entity – what are the
pieces of information that the record should include?
What entities will have relationships to each other? That is, what information will
need to be reassembled together when data is retrieved from the database?
Can you identify any possible many-to-many relationships between your tables
that will need to a resolving table?
